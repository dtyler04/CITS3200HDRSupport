What is GUIDE?

Students enrolled in a higher degree by research (HDR) at UWA are a diverse group, including PhD and Masters students who can commence at any time, switch between full-time and part-time, suspend studies, take annual leave, and study on or off campus. Some may seek support for neurodiversity and wellbeing.

Currently, notifications about research training events, resources, and support are sent to all students, but are not tailored to individual needs. Ideally, communication should consider each student's course, stage of candidature, FT/PT status, location, and optional information about neurodiversity and wellbeing.

Hence Group 19 has been tasked with creating: GUIDE
